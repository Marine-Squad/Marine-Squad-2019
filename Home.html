Frequently Asked Questions


1. How frequent are the workshops and what is the commitment like?

It is on a daily basis depending on the workshop itself. Some are once a week and some are biweekly, but most are once in 2 weeks. Don't worry about not being able to commit fully. You may choose your preferred dates!


2. I am a student and I don't have enough money to pay for the workshops?

It is alright! We have student rates too! We also have a 'pay it forward' system where by someone can pay on behalf of others who can't afford our classes.
